My Projects

1) Next-Gen Heart Monitoring


Comprehensive Health Alerts via GSM and Mobile Integration


Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) are a leading cause of death worldwide, often going undetected due to delayed diagnosis. This project develops a wearable heart monitoring system using sensors, microcontrollers, and GSM technology to provide real-time health alerts via SMS, even in remote areas.


Case Study: A rural heart patient suffered unnoticed complications. A GSM-based alert system could have prevented severe deterioration by notifying caregivers early.


The system includes an Arduino Uno, MAX30102 pulse oximeter, DS18B20 temperature sensor, and SIM800L GSM module, ensuring continuous monitoring without internet dependency.

2) Instant Blood Donation App


A Mobile Application for Real-Time Matching of Blood Donors and Recipients


Blood shortages cause delays in emergency treatments and surgeries due to the difficulty of finding suitable donors quickly. The Instant Blood Donation App solves this by using GPS-based real-time matching to connect donors with recipients instantly.


Case Study: The 2015 Nepal earthquake highlighted the urgent need for real-time donor tracking to prevent avoidable deaths caused by blood shortages.


The app features real-time donor tracking, emergency alerts, and Google Maps integration, ensuring fast access to blood. By enhancing donor participation, this app can revolutionize emergency response.
